Aftercare Instructions

Once you get your new tattoo, there is a set of guidelines you must follow to take care of your fresh tattoo. If you do not follow these instructions, your tattoo is at risk of infection.

  1. Leave bandage on for at least two hours. If saniderm is used, leave on for 24 hours.
  2. Wash with warm water and unscented, liquid soap.
  3. Pat dry with a paper towel.
  4. Gently apply thin layer of ointment.
  5. Repeat 3-4 times a day for a week.
  6. After first week, apply a thin layer of unscented lotion 4-6 times a day for at least the next two weeks.

Do not pick, peel, scratch, shave, soak, or tan a new tattoo. If you have any questions or concerns please contact me.
